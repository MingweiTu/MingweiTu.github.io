HELOC Risk Performance Prediction

The project objective is to develop a predictive model and a decision support system that evaluates the risk of Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC) applications. Our team trained different models and improved model performance with the accuracy of 71.86%. Our task in this project is to analyze the conjoint data and make recommendations about the product line for EarlyRider. Our team discovered customer segmentation with cluster analysis and causal analysis to generate our product pool; simulated the market to calculate profits for each scenario and found the most profitable product line to launch.
